Borrower Information

Understanding borrower profiles and requirements in Uganda

Borrower Requirements

  • Must be registered in a Ugandan group
  • Provide 2 guarantors with Ugandan phone numbers
  • Valid Ugandan National ID required
  • Good rating (3+ stars) to join multiple groups
  • Maximum 4 groups per borrower
  • No subscription fees for borrowers
